Step 1: Story Playthrough & Missables

Play on any difficulty (can be changed anytime). You can skip puzzles from the pause menu without voiding trophies. Focus on finishing the story and keep the one missable trophy in mind.

Details

MISSABLE! Found on Planet 2: Sargasso. Always kill these big dinosaurs when you see them. There are more than 5, but spawns change.

Easy Farm: On your first visit, kill the first optional Grunthor you find. After killing it, jump into the swamp water to die. You will respawn, and the Grunthor will be back. Repeat 5 times to get the trophy easily and not worry about it later.

Step 2: Collectibles & Mop-Up

After the story, select "Resume" to be placed before the final boss. You can now free-roam to all planets to clean up remaining collectibles and miscellaneous trophies.

Step 3: Challenge Mode (New Game+)

From the Title Screen, start Challenge Mode. You only need to play for about 10 minutes to reach the first Ms. Zurkon vendor.
